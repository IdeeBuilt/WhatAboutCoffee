Our Story

More Than
Just Coffee.

What About Coffee? was born from the belief that a great cafe should feel like home. We're community-first — collaborating with local artists, hosting events, building friendships, and serving every cup with intention.

From our plant-forward food menu to our sustainability initiatives, everything we do is rooted in purpose. Because coffee is better when it means something.
